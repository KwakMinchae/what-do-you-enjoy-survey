# What Do You Enjoy? — Activity Preference Survey

A multilingual, psychology-backed preference survey designed for seniors at Active Ageing Centres (AACs) in Singapore. Built as part of NUS GEN2062Y: Community Activities for Seniors.

🔗 **Live site:** [kwakminchae.github.io/what-do-you-enjoy-survey](https://kwakminchae.github.io/what-do-you-enjoy-survey/)

---

## Background

A common challenge in volunteer-led AAC sessions is activity mismatch — volunteers struggle to gauge what seniors actually want to do, especially when a language barrier limits free conversation. This survey provides a structured workaround: a short, low-friction questionnaire that surfaces activity preferences without requiring verbal back-and-forth.

---

## How it works

The survey is grounded in **Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan, 1985, 2000)**, one of the most empirically validated motivation frameworks in psychology. It measures three core psychological needs:

| Dimension | What it captures | Questions |
|---|---|---|
| **Autonomy** | Desire to create and express freely | Q1–5 |
| **Competence** | Desire to learn and improve | Q6–10 |
| **Relatedness** | Desire to connect with others | Q11–15 |

Seniors rate 15 statements on a 5-point Likert scale. Their scores across the three dimensions are compared to generate a **preference profile** with tailored activity suggestions.

### Profiles

| Profile | Dominant dimension | Example activities |
|---|---|---|
| The Creator | Autonomy | Crafts, painting, cooking |
| The Learner | Competence | Trivia, health talks, board games |
| The Social Bee | Relatedness | Group games, sing-alongs, storytelling |
| The Social Maker | Autonomy + Relatedness | Group cooking, collaborative crafts |
| The Social Learner | Competence + Relatedness | Group quizzes, shared learning |
| The Independent Achiever | Autonomy + Competence | Solo crafts with challenge, puzzles |
| The All-Rounder | All equal | Rotate freely |

## Features

- 15 Likert-scale questions across 3 SDT dimensions
- 4 languages: English, 中文, Bahasa Melayu, தமிழ்
- Auto-scored with visual result breakdown
- Activity chip suggestions per profile
- Peer-reviewed citations displayed on result page
- Single-file, zero dependencies — works offline
